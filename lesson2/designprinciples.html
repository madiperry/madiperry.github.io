Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

The Church's website shows visual hierarchy well. The most recent message is usually the biggest, and then it goes to the other messages. It also has the header, and the search bar in palces that you follow easily.

Contrast

Target's mobile website has great contrast. the bright red at the top, contrasts nicely wiht the grey and white of the rest of the screen.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple does a great job of illustraiting the white space and clean designs. With the simple designs in the center of the page, it is easy to see what the product is and to navigate the page.
